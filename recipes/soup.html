Vegetable Soup

Description

This versatile vegetable soup in a soup maker using your favorite vegetables. Serve with a dollop of creme fraîche and some herbs.

Ingredients

  • 200g vegetables, such as onions, celery and carrots, chopped
  • 300g potato, peeled and chopped
  • 700ml vegetable stock
  • crème fraîche and fresh herbs, to serve

Steps

  1. Put the vegetables, potatoes, and stock into a soup blender, and press the ‘smooth soup’ function. Make sure you don’t fill the soup maker above the max fill line.
  2. Once the cycle is complete, season, and pour into bowls with a dollop of crème fraîche and some herbs.
