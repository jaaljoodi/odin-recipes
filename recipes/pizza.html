Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Make the pizza dough.
  2. Make the pizza sauce.
  3. Prepare the cheese.
  4. Bake the pizza.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the pizza sauce over the dough.
  6. Use the pizza peel to carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone.
  7. Allow the pizza to cool for a minute or two before adding the basil on top (whole leaves, lightly torn, or thinly sliced).
